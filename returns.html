Returns and Refund Policy for

KALO, LLC [Consultants]

Policies for Ordering, Shipping and Returns

Ordering

All prices on this site are listed at the Retail rate. Anyone may review the content of the site and shop at their own pace. Anyone wishing to shop through this site, may set up an account and will be given option for discounted or other promotional items.

Refund Policy
KALO, LLC will offer a 90% return policy on items returned in re-saleable condition. KALO, LLC also offers a sampling program for people to try our products prior to making a commitment to purchase larger quantities. This takes the place of a traditional "money back guarantee." We encourage all people to sample our products prior to making any commitments to purchase larger volumes of product.

Cooling-off Period
KALO, LLC Consultants must inform Retail Customers of their right to rescind a purchase or an order within 72 hours, and ensure that the date of the order or purchase is entered on the order form. A Retail Customer who makes a purchase of $25.00 or more has three business days (Saturdays constitute business days) after the sale or execution of a contract to cancel the order and receive a full refund. Alaska residents have ten days.

Returns by Consultants (Products Returned by Retail Customers)
If a Retail Customer returns a product to the Consultant from whom it was purchased, the Consultant may return it to KALO, LLC for an exchange or refund (less shipping and handling and a possible restocking fee).

All products returned by Retail Customers must be returned to KALO, LLC within ten days from the date on which it was returned to the Consultant.

Return of Inventory and Sales Aids by Consultant
Upon cancellation of a KALO, LLC Consultant's Agreement, the Consultant may return inventory and sales aids for a refund if the product is in resalable condition. Upon receipt of the products and sales aids, the Consultant will be reimbursed 90% of the net cost of the original purchase price(s), less shipping and handling charges. If the purchases were made through a credit or debit card, the refund will be credited back to the same account. KALO, LLC shall deduct from the reimbursement paid to the Consultant any commissions, bonuses, rebates or other incentives.

Returns Procedure from Consultants to KALO, LLC
All returned products must have a Return Goods Authorization Number which is obtained by calling the KALO, LLC Customer Service Department. The Return Goods Authorization Number must be written on each carton returned.
